Welcome to the Open Access Button

How to use the Button:

To use the Button simply click the app the next time you can't afford to read an academic research paper (due to a "paywall" or "firewall"). If you're unsure we've provided a few example pages where you should hit the Button.


What happens next?

Once you've pushed the Button we will help you find alternative, free copies of the paper. If nothing is available add the paper to your wish list and we'll employ a number of other ways to find your access.
We will notify you by email if we make progress.
